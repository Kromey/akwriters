Adium is our recommended chat client for Mac users.

Setting up Adium

Once you have downloaded and installed Adium, add a new account by opening the File menu, going to the Add Account sub-menu, and choosing {% prosody_proto %} (Jabber).

To configure your account, set your options as so:

  1. Jabber ID: {{ user.username|default:"Your username" }}@{% prosody_domain %}
  2. Password: Your password

Under Personal, give yourself an alias; this only shows up to you, and identifies your own messages. I like to set it to "Me".

Now click the OK button to finish and connect to the server.

Joining the Conversation

The first time you sign in, Annika will ask to be your friend. Annika is our chat bot, and has a growing number of useful talents. You should accept her request, and then ask her for help (simply say "help" in a chat window with her) to start learning what she can do to help your writing.

You can also jump straight into the group chat by simply asking Annika to invite you (i.e. type "invite me" in a chat window with her).
