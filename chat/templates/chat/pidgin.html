Once you have downloaded and installed Pidgin, add a new account by opening the Accounts menu and selecting Manage Accounts, and then click the "Add..." button.

To configure your account, set your options as so:

  1. Protocol: XMPP
  2. Username: {{ user.username|default:"Your username" }}
  3. Domain: fairbanksnano.org
  4. Resource: Optionally identify this location to yourself.

Under User Options, give yourself a Local alias; this only shows up to you, and identifies your own messages. I like to set it to "Me".

Now click the Add button to finish and connect to the server.

You may see an error about an invalid certificate. This is fine; a "real" certificate costs a lot of money, so I'm currently using a "self-signed" one instead. Just click the Accept button to continue connecting.

Joining the Conversation

The first time you sign in, Annika will ask to be your friend. Annika is our chat bot, and has a growing number of useful talents. You should accept her request, and then ask her for help (simply say "help" in a chat window with her) to start learning what she can do to help your writing.

[Images coming soon.] I also strongly suggest you join the Nano chat room. To do so, open your Tools menu and select Room List. Choose your fairbanksnano.org username from the list at the top, then click on Get List. Select the "nano" room, then click on the "Add Chat" button to create a "bookmark" for the room in your buddy list. Now you can quickly and easily join the room every time you sign on.
